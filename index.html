BE is a Nonprofit Organisation.

We design products and services alongside organizations that are committed to creating a more just and inclusive world.

Our theory of change is to nurture a critical mass of inspiring stories which will lead to a movement of grassroots entrepreneurship. We want to disrupt the status quo by creating an opportunity for leadership and social endeavor among people whose lived reality and its challenges drive them for change in various socio-economic hurdles.

Work

Learning Journeys

We design experiential learning programs to nurture the next generation of leaders & entrepreneurs. Programs facilitate & enable young people to explore themselves, understand the world better with empathy, build problem-solving abilities and inspire them to create the road-map of their entrepreneurial journey. The community of the Alumni then sustain the energy.

Our Leadership Framework

Framework engages the strengths of Head, Heart & Hand at 3 levels of human engagement - Self, Social & System. Strengths of Heart enable harmonious relationships with other people. Strengths of Head enable independent thinking. Strengths of Hand enable us to achieve goals. Nine Core Competencies encompasses a diverse range of essential leadership skills, behaviors & values to nurture the beings at different stages of the learning journey.
